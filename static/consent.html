Consent Information

We are asking you to be in a research study. Participating in the study is voluntary. This form gives you information about the study. Please read this carefully; ask questions if you have them by emailing tschweiz AT cs.washington.edu; and then decide whether or not you want to participate in the study.

PURPOSE OF THE STUDY

The purpose of the study is to evaluate how a neural network-based tool can help programmers complete file system tasks in Bash.

STUDY PROCEDURES

The experiment starts with a short training session familiarize you with the task-solving workflow and the tools. Then, you will be asked to write bash one-liners to complete file system tasks. The experiment concludes with a short survey. The experiment will take approximately 60 minutes to complete.

RISKS, STRESS, OR DISCOMFORT

You may experience discomfort characteristic of the regular usage of a computer system. Should you experience discomfort, you may stop at any time.

BENEFITS OF THE STUDY

Short-term. You will be financially compensated for your time. For completing the experiment, you will receive a $15 Amazon gift card. The gift card will be sent at most two weeks after your participation in the experiment.
Long-term. ​The findings from this research may benefit future software tools.

OTHER INFORMATION

You may refuse to participate and you are free to withdraw from this study at any time without penalty or loss of benefits to which you are otherwise entitled.
