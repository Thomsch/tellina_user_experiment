Consent Information

We are asking you to be in a research study. This form gives you information to help you decide whether or not to be in the study. Being in the study is voluntary. Please read this carefully. You may ask any questions about the study. Then you can decide whether or not you want to be in the study.

PURPOSE OF THE STUDY

The purpose of the study is to evaluate how deep neural network-based recommender systems can help people achieve their daily tasks. For this study, we look at how a natural language-to-bash recommender can help programmers complete file system tasks in Bash.

STUDY PROCEDURES

The experiment starts with a short training session to get you familiarized with the recommender system and the task solving workflow. Then, you will be asked to complete two series of tasks where you have to write bash one-liners to complete file system tasks. The experiment concludes with a small survey through which we receive your feedback. The study will take approximately 1.5 hours of your time.

RISKS, STRESS, OR DISCOMFORT

You may experience discomfort characteristic of the regular usage of a computer system. Should you experience any discomfort, you may stop at any time.

BENEFITS OF THE STUDY

Short-term. You will be financially compensated for your time. For participating in the entire study, you will be compensated with a $15 Amazon gift card. The gift card will be sent at most two weeks after your participation in the experiment.
Long-term. ​The findings from this research may benefit future software tools in various domains.

OTHER INFORMATION

You may refuse to participate and you are free to withdraw from this study at any time without penalty or loss of benefits to which you are otherwise entitled.
